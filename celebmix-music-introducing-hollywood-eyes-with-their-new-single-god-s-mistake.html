CelebMix Music Introducing: Hollywood Eyes With Their New Single 'God's Mistake'

Here at CelebMix, we love nothing more than introducing our readers to new, up and coming talent. In this particular article, we introduce you to four-piece Cheshire-based pop/indie band Hollywood Eyes.

The band comprises of members Ryan Pridding (vocalist/guitar), Anthony Edes (guitarist), Arthur Jones (bassist) and Christopher Mee (drummer). Members Ryan and Anthony first started the band by making demos, before recruiting remaining members Arthur and Christopher on bass and drums.

Their first single ‘Wanna Be’ was released back in May 2018 and since then, they have really found their unique sound. While drawing on a mixture of influences from different music genres, the band are becoming renowned for their pop/indie sound and creating music that makes them stand out in the competitive industry today.

Hollywood Eyes’ new single ‘God’s Mistake’ has an uptempo nature throughout and is infectiously catchy. This track showcases not only phenomenal vocals, but the talent of the band and they are definitely ones to look out for in 2019, as continued exciting things lie in store for the band throughout the year.

Hollywood Eyes have been described as the ‘full package’a band to see and hear.’

In addition to the recent release of their new single, the band are also set to headline their own show at Liverpool’s Sound Basement on 02 February 2019. Tickets for this event can be bought by clicking here.
