Playlist: CelebMix Lazy Sundays No. 25 - 1D Day

Happy Sunday ladies and gents! Welcome back to a special edition CelebMix Lazy Sundays Spotify playlist! It’s been a minute, but boy do we have a treat for you! Eager to know the details? Read on to find out!

In case you haven’t read the title, this weekend’s playlist is all things One Direction, making today 1D Day. The band celebrates 7 years as a band today, and even though there have been a couple ups and downs plus a hiatus, that’s not stopping fans from celebrating the band’s anniversary worldwide.

Since it’s a rather special day, we too are celebrating the day but in our own way of course, and bring to you a special edition weekend playlist full of 1D hits and our personal favourites. From their very first album to their fifth, you’ll find 6 tracks from each album and get to hear the band’s progression in music over the last couple years!

Are you ready for songs that will hit you right in the feels? Listen to this weekend’s special edition playlist now. As always, don’t forget to tell us which 1D songs you loved best over on our Twitter (@CelebMix).
