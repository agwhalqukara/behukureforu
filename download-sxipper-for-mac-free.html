Download Sxipper for Mac - Free

Sxipper is a free program designed for Mac users that aims to simplify the process of remembering and filling in usernames and passwords while browsing the internet using Firefox. With the increasing number of online accounts, it can be a daunting task to keep track of all the login credentials. Sxipper offers a solution by auto-completing usernames and passwords, making it easier and more efficient to access websites.

One of the key features of Sxipper is its ability to learn as you type. It detects keywords related to your address or other personal information and automatically fills in the entire form based on the empty fields. This saves time and effort, especially when dealing with multiple forms on different websites. However, it's worth noting that Sxipper may not always fill in all forms correctly, particularly if the form contains miscellaneous fields.

While Firefox already offers a built-in auto-complete feature, Sxipper provides a more comprehensive and secure solution. It eliminates the need to remember numerous usernames and passwords or resort to less secure methods such as writing them down. Sxipper is not perfect, but it offers a significant improvement in managing login credentials.

Overall, Sxipper is a useful tool for Firefox users who want the convenience of auto-completing usernames and passwords without compromising their security. It streamlines the login process and reduces the time and effort required to access online accounts.
