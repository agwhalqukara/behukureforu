Who is Danny Koker? Short Wiki/Bio

Daniel Nicholas Koker II was born on 5 January 1964 under the birth sign of Capricorn, in Cleveland, Ohio USA and is currently aged 55. He spent his childhood divided between Cleveland and Detroit, where he was raised alongside his sister, Kim Koker, by his father, Daniel Koker Sr., who was an official for the Ford Motor Company, and his mother, Mary Koker.

Career Beginnings

As a young boy, Danny developed his love for cars, and later learned to restore them, to earn enough money for himself.

His career began when the family bought the Channel 33 network, based in Las Vegas, so he started appearing in the show “Count Cool Rider”, and eventually became the host of the show “Saturday Fright At The Movies”. As time passed, he gained enormous experience and improved his skills, so he could launch his own restoration shop, called “Count’s Kustoms”, after which he was invited to join the cast of the History Channel reality series “Pawn Stars” in 2010.

It was followed by his appearances in another show, entitled “American Restoration”, between 2011 and 2014, which added a considerable amount to his net worth.

Rise to Fame: “Counting Cars” and His Own Car Collection

However, Danny’s career in the entertainment industry reached its zenith in August of 2012, when he created and became the main cast of the History Channel reality television series “Counting Cars”. The show follows his daily activities at “Count’s Kustoms” and is currently in its eighth season, increasing further his wealth.
