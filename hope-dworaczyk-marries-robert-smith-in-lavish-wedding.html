Hope Dworaczyk Marries Robert Smith in Lavish Wedding

To the next chapter! Playboy model and TV personality Hope Dworaczyk and her fiance, Robert Smith, have tied the knot, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm. The couple said “I do” in a lavish wedding at Villa Cimbrone in Ravello, Italy on the Amalfi Coast on Saturday, July 25.

Dworaczyk, 30, and Smith, 50, were feted by 250 guests and wrote their own vows, a guest tells Us. The blushing bride got ready for her special day with the help of makeup artist and good friend Joyce Bonelli. She and her seven bridesmaids also wore custom Naeem Khan dresses. For the reception, Dworaczyk wore a custom Galia Lahav gown.

The magical night was filled with many surprises. The guest tells Us that John Legend performed “For the First Time” when Dworaczyk walked down the aisle. In addition, the couple’s adorable son Hendrix, 7 months, made his way to the altar on a cloud rigged by the Cirque du Soleil production team.

“We both love throwing great parties,” Dworaczyk told Us ahead of her dream wedding. “We do them a couple times a year. We’re all in when it comes to creating things so he’s my partner in crime for party planning.”
