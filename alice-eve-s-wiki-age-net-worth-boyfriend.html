Alice Eves Wiki, Age, Net Worth, Boyfriend.

Alice Eve is a British-born American actress. She first started appearing in movies when she was 22. To date, she has starred in numerous films and TV shows, most notably “She’s Out of My League (2010)” and “Men in Black 3 (2012)”.

Another interesting fact about her is that she has a unique condition called heterochromia, which has led to her right eye being blue while the left eye appears green in color.  Let’s learn more about Alice Eve in this subsequent review.

Alice Eve Wiki.

Alice Eve is a television personality and Hollywood star currently based in America. She was born on 6th February 1982, in London, and is the daughter of Trevor Eve and Sharon Maughan. Interestingly, her parents are also professional actors. Alice

Eve’s acting career began while she was a student at a Los Angeles’ based University. She took a gap year, which enabled her to take classes at the Beverly Hills playhouse. What followed was an audition for the role of Grace Maclean in “The Horse Whisperer (1998).” However, the role went to another rising star, Scarlett Johansson.

This did not deter Alice’s ambitions of becoming a professional actress. In 2006, she appeared in two films, “Tom Stoppard” and “rock and roll” at the royal court theatre.

These were both directed by British theater director Trevor Bunn. A look into his biography revealed that he also works for the Royal Shakespeare Company. The following year, Alice Eve was nominated for best supporting actress for her role in Broadway transfer at Theatregoers’ Choice Awards.

Alice Eve, an English-American actress

Additionally, Alice has also featured on the annual Independent critics’ list of the 100 most beautiful faces around the world a few times.  In 2017, she joined the cast of Netflix’s Iron Fist, playing Mary Walker’s role. Despite being a British native, Alice became an American citizen by naturalization in 2017.

Net worth.

It comes as no surprise that Alice has built her wealth on the foundation of an impressive acting career. Her 2010 Rom-Com, She’s Out of my League, for instance, raked in $49.8 million at the worldwide box office.  Eve has also worked on Startrek: Into Darkness, a film that made $467 million at the box office. As of 2021, Alice Eve’s net worth is $6 million.

Alice Eve had quite an eventful relationship with renowned poet Adam O’Riordan. He has won several plaudits and awards, including the prestigious Somerset Maugham. Alice and Adam first met in Oxford. A first date quickly transitioned into a long-term relationship.
