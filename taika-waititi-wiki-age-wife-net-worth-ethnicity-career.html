Taika Waititi Biography

Taika Waititi was born on New Zealand’s North Island on August 16, 1975. His mother, Robin Cohen, is of European descent and works as a teacher. He’s the son of a Maori farmer who used to labor in ‘Te Whanau—Apanui.’ When he was five years old, his parents divorced, and his mother raised him.

Initially, he went by his mother’s surname Cohen but later changed to Waititi. He went to Onslow College for secondary school. In 1997, he received a BA in theatre from the Victoria University of Wellington.
