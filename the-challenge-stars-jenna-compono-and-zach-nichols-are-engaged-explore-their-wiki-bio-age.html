First Encounter

Jenna Compono and Zach Nichols first met each other while battling it out on ‘Battle of the Exes II’. From then on, a spark developed between the two.

Until Rivals III in 2016, the pair were dating on and off subsequently splitting. Later, during Invasion of the Champions, Jenna and Zach saw each other. However, they didn’t get enough time together due to his early ouster.

In 2017, Jenna and Zach rekindled their romance post-Champs vs. Stars.
