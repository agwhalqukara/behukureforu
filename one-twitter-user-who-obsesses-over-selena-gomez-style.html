One Twitter User Who Obsesses Over Selena Gomez Style

Actress/singer Selena Gomez has been fortunate enough to have a dedicated fan base that has struck with her through ups and downs.  When Gomez broke up with her fellow pop star ex-boyfriend, Justin Bieber, her fans rallied around her in support.  Last year, when Gomez announced that she would be cancelling the Australian and Asian legs of her Stars Dance Tour, countless disappointed fans continued to support Gomez as she took some time out of the spotlight to deal with her personal issues. This year, Gomez appears to be rejuvenated and ready to dedicate all of her energy to her fans.  In November she released a compilation album that includes solo work and songs from her band, Selena Gomez & The Scene.  However, while Gomez seems to be primarily focused on her music career, one of her most dedicated fans seems to be more fixated on something else.  One Twitter user has repeatedly and shamelessly expressed his love for Selena Gomez’ impeccable style and hopes to one day get some personal pointers from the star. Here are five tweets from a young man who is obsessed with Selena Gomez’ style.

5.

i like your style it makes me wild@Selenagomez

— Jotellersd (@JotellerSd) July 1, 2014

The simplest compliments are usually the most heartfelt, and this Twitter user conveys his message to Selena Gomez in just one sentence.

4.

i really love her style
i think she could give me styling tips because she s my styling angel my styling queen @Selenagomez ~ Jotellersd

— Jotellersd (@JotellerSd) February 1, 2014

Not only is Selena Gomez an angel, but this Twitter user believes that she is a queen as well.  Perhaps one day she will actually be able to grant @JotellerSd’s wish and share some of her styling tips with him

3.

@selenagomez
I like your style you makes me wild
You looks so great Everytime I see your face you put me in a state a state of shock girl

— Jotellersd (@JotellerSd) November 22, 2014

@JotellerSd has added to his original “I like your style it makes me wild tweet,” with a few more song-like lines. It appears that this young man enjoys being driven wild and put into a state of shock

2.

i wish i was Selena gomez
because she s pretty i love her style her face her body her dance moves i love her at all
#SelenaForMMVA

— Jotellersd (@JotellerSd) June 13, 2014

Nothing about Selena Gomez is lost on @JotellerSd as he proudly tweets about loving all of her.

1.

@selenagomez I love your style your fashion style and I wish you would shave my hair someday because I believe and trust in you my angel

— Jotellersd (@JotellerSd) November 4, 2014

While Selena Gomez is good at many things, I doubt most people would trust her with the task of cutting their hair.  However, @JotellerSd seems to believe that Gomez is an angel capable of all things.
