Malika Haqq, Khloe Kardashian's Best Friend, Arrested for DUI

Party's over. Malika Haqq was arrested for DUI in L.A. on Thursday, Oct. 8, Us Weekly can confirm.

The reality star — and Khloe Kardashian's best friend — crashed her black Mercedes Benz on the 101 freeway at 3:01 a.m., a spokesperson for the California Highway Patrol tells Us. Her vehicle was blocking lanes north of Vineland.

Haqq, 32, was arrested for DUI and was booked at the Van Nuys Police Station. In a CHP report, it states that she showed "objective symptoms of intoxication." She was later released Thursday with her bail set at $30,000.
