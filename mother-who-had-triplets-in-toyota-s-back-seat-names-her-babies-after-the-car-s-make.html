Mother who had triplets in Toyota's back seat names her babies after the car's make

Three new-born children were named after the car they were born in - a Toyota Avast. The Indonesian mother didn't make it to hospital in time and then decided to name her children after the car's model.

An Indonesian mother decided to name her new-born babies after the car they were born in.

Mumun Muafiyah lives about 25km away from the hospital and didn't make it there in time to deliver her children.

In fact, the Mumun only made it 5km before her first child was born. Then, 5km later, her next baby was born. A surprised and exhausted Mumun gave birth to her third child about 5 minutes later. She was expecting to give birth to twins.

The mother and her three new-born babies were taken to the hospital in the city of Cilegon where they were all given medical care.

News24 reports that the mother and her children were all healthy.

Because surnames aren't used in Indonesia, the parents can choose whole names for their children.

Mumun chose to name her children after the Toyota Avast they were born in.

The children's names are: Nazrina Khaula Avanza, Nazrina Khaira Avanza, and Nazrina Haura Avanza.
