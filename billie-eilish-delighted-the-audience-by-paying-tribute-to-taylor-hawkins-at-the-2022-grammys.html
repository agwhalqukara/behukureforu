Billie Eilish delighted the audience by paying tribute to Taylor Hawkins at the 2022 Grammys

Billy Elish wore a black T-shirt with a picture of Taylor Hawkins performing two recent songs, including “Everything I Wanted”.

Billie Eilish was the first artist to pay tribute to drummer Taylor Hawkins, a member of the rock band Foo Fighters, during the 2022 Grammy Awards gala this Sunday.

The singer-songwriter performed two recent songs, including “Everything I Wanted”, while wearing a black T-shirt featuring Hawkins, who died last Friday, March 25, in Colombia at the age of 50. 

A brief tribute of Eilish, who kissed his shirt at the end of his act, entertained the audience and added three awards with which his band rocked the event, as the Foo Fighters won the best rock Performances (“Making a Fire”), Best Rock Song (“Waiting for War”) and Best Rock Album (Madicine at Midnight).
