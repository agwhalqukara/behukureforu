Daveed Diggs Biography

Daveed Diggs (born January 24, 1982) was born to his parents in Oakland, California as Daveed Daniele Diggs. His father’s name is Dountes Diggs, and his mother’s name is Barbara. Back then, his father was a bus driver and his mother was a social worker. Barbara was Jewish and Dountes was Afro-American. He was his parents’ only kid. Diggs now resides in Los Angeles, California.

Daveed finished his high school studies at Berkeley High School. Brown University awarded him a degree in Theater.

Daveed Diggs Height, Weight

Daveed Diggs has a height of 5 feet 10 inches. He weighs around 57 kg. He is well-liked by his female followers due to his attractive appearance and appealing curly hair.

Career

Daveed Diggs traveled to Oakland after graduating to join the hip-hop group Clipping. He was just experimenting at the time and had no clue that his career would take a dramatic turn after joining the group.

The group’s debut mixtape, named ‘Mid City,’ was published in 2013. They released EPs and LPs in the years that followed. One of their tracks, ‘the deep,’ was also nominated for an award. Daveed went on to work with other artists and record numerous well-known rap tracks.

His popularity rose when he appeared in the musical ‘Hamilton’ as Marquis de Lafayette and Thomas Jefferson. For his outstanding performance in the show, he received two honors. Furthermore, his rap song appears in the film Zootopia.

He has also appeared in a number of television programs. ‘Black-ish’ and ‘Unbreakable’ are two of the shows in which he has recurring parts. He has also featured in various moments from ‘The Get Down’. Daveed has also appeared in various television ads.

Daveed Diggs Girlfriend, Dating

Daveed Diggs is in a long-term relationship with Emmy Raver-Lampman. Fortunately, the friendship lasted just two or three years until the pair started dating in 2019. The pair were first spotted together on February 23rd, 2019, at the Film Independent Spirit Awards with their good friend Janina Gavankar.
