Pregnant Chrissy Teigen Looks Forward to Barfing, Eating Pizza Often

Tell Us all of your pregnancy confessions, Chrissy Teigen! The supermodel, who announced on Monday, Oct. 12, that she's expecting her first child with husband John Legend, tweeted the following day that she's looking forward to the ups and downs of pregnancy — cravings and morning sickness included.

I am so excited I could barf 4-5 times a day

— christine teigen (@chrissyteigen) October 13, 2015

I have come to realize that at some point in the day, I will order and consume a pizza so it might as well be in the morning

— christine teigen (@chrissyteigen) October 13, 2015

While thanking followers for their support after announcing the news, Teigen also managed to sneak in a tweet about Tamra Judge being baptized on the latest episode of The Real Housewives of Orange County.

Can we talk about tamara's rushing-water life flashback last night because it was the real story of the night

— christine teigen (@chrissyteigen) October 13, 2015

After struggling to conceive, Teigen, 29, ecstatically shared her pregnancy news via Instagram on Monday night. “John and I are so happy to announce that we are pregnant,” the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model wrote, alongside a snap of her husband, 36, resting his hand on her tiny bump.

"As many of you know, we’ve been trying to have a baby for a while now. It hasn’t been easy, but we kept trying because we can’t wait to bring our first child into the world and grow our family. We’re so excited that it’s finally happening. Thank you for all your love and well wishes. I look forward to all the belly touching!"

She even took a moment to reply to Us! After Us Weekly envisioned what her future child will look like on Tuesday, Teigen simply replied via Twitter with an "AH."
