Diddy Responds To 'Making The Band' Hate: Stop All Your Crying!!

“STOP ALL YOUR CRYING, BITCHING & MOANING. HUSTLE HARDER OR GET THE F*CK OUT OF OUR WAY. – LOVE,” he posted on Instagram Stories.

“I don’t even know how I started thinking like that. I done had it all and I fell to the bottom. How do you come back from that when you battling a giant? It ain’t got nothing to do with your skill no more ’cause your skill is 90% better than any n*gga that’s out there right now and the world knows it.
