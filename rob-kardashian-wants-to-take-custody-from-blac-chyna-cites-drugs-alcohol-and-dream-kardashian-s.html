Rob Kardashian Wants To Take Custody From Blac Chyna: Cites Drugs, Alcohol And Dream Kardashians

In a shocking new report, Rob Kardashian details why he is requesting primary custody of his daughter Dream Kardashian. The toddler’s behavior has allegedly changed for the worse!

Currently, the two share physical custody of the 3-year-old that can be seen on social media spending time with both Blac Chyna and the Kardashian clan. Rob has filed papers for his ex-fiance Chyna to only have weekend arrangements with a nanny present.

The former Keeping Up With the Kardashian’s star says that his daughter has been displaying aggressive behavior towards her cousins and peers in gymnastics class. Khloe Kardashian is backing him up stating that she has also noticed the change of behavior.

To make matters worse, the outcast brother claims that his baby mama is excessively drinking, has strangers frequently partying at her house, and using cocaine.

Dream is also allegedly copying gestures from her mom including ‘naked twerking, and acting out sexual positions’ according to the TMZ piece.

The father went on to claim that when she is returned to his home, she has dirty hair, nails, and teeth.

This comes after Blac Chyna recently completed an interview talking about co-parenting with her children’s fathers.
