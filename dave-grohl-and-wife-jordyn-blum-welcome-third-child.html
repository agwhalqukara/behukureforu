Dave Grohl and Wife Jordyn Blum Welcome Third Child

Dave Grohl is a dad again! The Foo Fighters frontman and his wife Jordyn Blum welcomed their third child, a baby girl named Ophelia Saint Grohl, on Aug. 1

The exciting news comes just days after less fortunate news — Grohl lost his father, James Grohl, on Aug. 6 to a longterm illness.
