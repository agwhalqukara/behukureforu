Anne Nicole Smiths Daughter Dannielynn, 14, Looks So Grown Up at Kentucky Derby 2021 with Dad Lar

Dannielynn Birkhead is making her annual appearance at the Kentucky Derby!

The 14-year-old daughter of the late Anna Nicole Smith stepped out for the 2021 Kentucky Derby with her dad Larry Birkhead on Friday afternoon (April 30) at the Churchill Downs racetrack in Louisville, Kentucky.

Larry, 48, took to Instagram to share a few photos of he and Dannielynn attending the annual event.

“It’s that time of year again. The @kentuckyderby weekend is here!” Larry wrote. “Even though our favorite event The Barnstable-Brown Party won’t be back until next year, we decided to head out on this beautiful day to Churchill Downs racetrack in preparation for The Kentucky Derby.”

In the photos, Dannielynn looked so grown up in a bright blue pantsuit by Jovani paired with a white floral headpiece and sparkling white ballet flats.

“Dannielynn ‘finished first’ in her @jovanifashions pant suit and a beautiful white flowered fascinator. She said she wanted to wear something entirely different than she had in the past,” Larry continued. “I guess that means no more ‘little girl’ dresses?? She cleaned out the chocolate brownies, ice cream and nachos while at the same time critiquing my outfit. ?‍♂️ #kentuckyderby weekend #kentuckyoaks #prouddad #timeflies #nomorebraces #jovanifashions”

Each year, the father-daughter duo attend Kentucky Derby events, where Larry and Anna met for the first time in 2003.

Anna, a former Playboy model and reality TV star, passed away in 2007.
