Happy 21st Birthday Michael Clifford

Today Michael Clifford turned 21. The 5SOS guitarist and his band mates are on a little break at the moment, and what better time to have a 21st birthday celebration?

Michael has achieved so much in his 21 years of life and in his career, we can not wait to see what he will achieve in the next 21. Going from a young boy in Australia wanting nothing more than to share his story through music, to one of the most famous guitarists in the industry right now selling out arenas around the world. Michael has touched the lives of so many fans in his career, helping them get through through times in their life. Fighting an ongoing battle with depression himself, Michael connects to those who are scared of the way they feel. Telling them it’s okay to not be okay. The connection they have isn’t something they or Michael take for granted.

The hashtag #HappyBirthdayMichael is trending on Twitter in numerous countries around the world. Tens of thousands of tweets have come in from fans wishing Michael the happiest and most wonderful birthday.
