Lauren Conrad: Brody Jenner &quot;Has a Lot of Experience&quot; With Sex

LC has the 411! Brody Jenner keeps busy behind closed doors — just ask Lauren Conrad. The Paper Crown fashion designer dished about her good friend’s radio show, Sex with Brody, in Cosmopolitan’s October 2015 issue.

“My husband [William Tell] told me [about it]. I didn’t know!” Conrad, 29, told the mag. Asked whether she would take his sex advice, she quipped: “He has a lot of experience … so sure, depending on what kind of advice you want.” (The former reality star and Jenner, 32, “dated” on MTV’s The Hills, but the DJ revealed back in July that their entire romantic relationship was scripted for the show, which ran from 2006 to 2010.)

Conrad isn’t as open about her own sex life. In February 2014, she had the perfect response when she was asked on Sway Calloway’s Sway in the Morning radio show what her favorite position is. “CEO,” she slyly said. Calloway shot back: “I like that! High-five! CEO. That was dope.”

The former MTV star also keeps mum about her ex BFF Heidi Montag. The former roommates famously ended their friendship when Montag and her now-husband, Spencer Pratt, allegedly spread rumors that Conrad had a sex tape.
