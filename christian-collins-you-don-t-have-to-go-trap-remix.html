Christian Collins You Dont Have To Go Trap Remix

Last December, Christian Collins released his third single “You Don’t Have To Go”. This downtempo electronic-R&B song put his smooth vocals in the spotlight. In contrast, the lyrics told a sad tale about a girl who can’t be pinned down. With its catchy beat and sound, the single easily makes it onto our repeat playlist.

Christian Collins has taken his hit single “You Don’t Have to Go” one step further by releasing a trap rework of it.

This trap remix, currently available on Spotify, contains a faster beat and a deeper bassline while still retaining the smooth chorus.

As a result, we get to enjoy a fun EDM track with heavy bass and mystic accents. The song holds up well to dancing, relaxing, and long car drives at night. Keep your eye out for this budding young artist on Twitter and YouTube, both of which include updates of his music career alongside his usual social media content.
