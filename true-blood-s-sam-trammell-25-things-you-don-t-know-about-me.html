True Blood's Sam Trammell: 25 Things You Don't Know About Me

Sam Trammell, 43, (True Blood airs on HBO Sundays at 9 p.m.), shares the 25 things you don't know about him with Us Weekly.

1. I once lived on an Indian reservation.

2. I love lemons and eat them whole.

3. I grew up next to a cemetary and always wished my house was haunted.

4. In high school, I played in a Rush cover band.

5. I've never worn a watch.

6. I can't give a decent toast to save my life.

7. I used to work as a proofreader at Merrill Lynch.

8. I still don't understand bonds.

9. As a kid, I had a crooked bowl cut.

10. I have a signed Evel Knievel photo that's rad.

11. I'm a vocabulary nerd.

12. I like maps.

13. I try to be late, but I'm always early.

14. I don't like hot dogs.

15. I pierced my ear in high school. It has since closed.

16. The first car I ever bought was a 1978 Camaro Z28.

17. I can't stop listening to NPR's This American Life.

18. I always get sick right before a job starts.

19. I used to collect old political buttons.

20. I once stole a moped in Florence, Italy — then returned it.

21. I've spent time hiking the Appalachian Trail with my dad.

22. I love spicy food.
